Anderson

The East Side's Music School.

The Anderson location sits in the Crossings of Anderson plaza at the intersection of Beechmont Avenue and 8 Mile Road. Families come here for private lessons, welcoming studios, and front desk support on the days we are open, serving Anderson Township, Mt. Washington, Newtown, and the surrounding East Side communities.

Anderson students are part of the full CSM community, with private lessons, recital opportunities, teacher matching, and support from the same local school families know across Greater Cincinnati.

Why Families Come Here

Good Teachers. Convenient Hours. Consistent Scheduling.

01
Available seven days a week
Lessons run Monday through Sunday. Siblings can schedule back-to-back at the same location — a practical detail that saves a lot of driving for families with more than one student.

Month-to-month enrollment
No semester minimums. No long-term commitment required to start. Enrollment is month-to-month.
