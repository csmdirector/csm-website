Holiday Schedule & Closures

CSM observes a set of scheduled closures each year for major holidays and breaks. No lessons are held on these dates. Monthly tuition is adjusted accordingly — you will not be charged for any scheduled closure dates.

Halloween — October 31: CSM will be open on Saturday, October 31. Lessons run on their normal schedule.

Tuition for any month containing a scheduled closure is calculated based on the number of lessons held that month. If a closure falls on your regular lesson day, that lesson will not be held and will not appear on your Opus1 schedule. No action is required — your account adjusts automatically.

If you have questions about your schedule or tuition for a specific month, contact your location directly or log in to your Opus1 account.

View your upcoming lesson schedule at cincinnatischoolofmusic.opus1.io — all scheduled and upcoming lessons are visible in your account.
