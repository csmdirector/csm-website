Common Questions

Things Gift-Givers Often Ask

What happens after I purchase?
The certificate is delivered by email immediately — to the recipient if you'd like, or to you to forward or print and present yourself. You can include a personal message. The recipient holds onto the certificate until they're ready to schedule their first lesson.
What can a gift certificate be used for?
Private music lessons in any instrument we teach — piano, voice, guitar, drums, strings, woodwinds, and more. Redeemable at any of our five Greater Cincinnati locations with one of our 60+ teachers, subject to availability.
What's the difference between Silver and Gold?
Silver is the four-lesson package — a good start, enough to introduce someone to music lessons. Gold is the eight-lesson package — a great start, with enough time to actually begin. Both cover 30-minute private lessons in any instrument we teach, at any of our five Greater Cincinnati locations.
How are lessons scheduled?
The recipient (or parent) simply contacts our office to get scheduled and have the gift certificate applied.
When does a gift certificate expire?
Gift certificates are valid for one year from the date of purchase. The recipient should plan to schedule their first lesson within that window.
