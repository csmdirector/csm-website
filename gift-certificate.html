Gift Options

A Simple Way to Start Someone in Music.

A Cincinnati School of Music gift certificate gives someone the chance to start — or continue — private music lessons without any up-front commitment from them. It covers a month of lessons for any instrument we teach, at any location that works for their schedule.

Certificates are delivered digitally and can be redeemed by contacting any CSM location. The recipient chooses their instrument, location, and lesson schedule. No contracts. Month-to-month from there if they want to keep going.

Silver — One Month
One month of private lessons
Four lessons for any instrument at any CSM location. Covers piano, voice, guitar, drums, strings, woodwinds, and more. Valid for students of any age or skill level.
Gold — Two Months
Two months of private lessons
Eight lessons across two months. Enough time to find a groove, settle into a routine, and see what consistent instruction does for a student. The most popular gift option.
