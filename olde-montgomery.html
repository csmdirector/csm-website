Montgomery Location

Music Lessons Close to Home

Our Montgomery location on Montgomery Road has been part of the north Cincinnati community since Cincinnati School of Music opened in 2012. Families from Montgomery, Blue Ash, Symmes Township, Kenwood, Indian Hill, and the surrounding area come here every week for private instruction in piano, voice, guitar, drums, violin, and winds.

Montgomery is one of five CSM locations across Greater Cincinnati. Every lesson is private. Every teacher is here because they are good at teaching — not just good at music. Every student has a consistent weekly slot that becomes a reliable part of the family schedule.
