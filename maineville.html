Maineville

Maineville's Music School.

The Maineville location sits along OH-48 and serves families throughout Warren County — Maineville, Waynesville, Morrow, Lebanon, and communities north and south along the corridor. Families come here for private lessons in welcoming studios, with office support available on the days we are open.

Maineville students are part of the full CSM community, with private lessons, recital opportunities, teacher matching, and the same local support families know across our other locations.

Why Families Come Here

Good Teachers. Convenient Hours. Consistent Scheduling.

01
Available six days a week
Lessons run Monday through Saturday. Siblings can schedule back-to-back at the same location — a practical detail that saves a lot of driving for families with more than one student.
02
Month-to-month enrollment
No semester minimums. No long-term commitment required to start. Enrollment is month-to-month.
03
Office staff on site every day
Scheduling, billing, recital information, enrollment questions — our office team is available every day we are open. You will reach a person, not a voicemail box.
