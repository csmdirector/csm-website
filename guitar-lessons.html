Guitar Lessons in Cincinnati

For beginner to advanced students, ages 6 and up. Private instruction at five Greater Cincinnati locations.

Book Intro
Guitar at CSM

Private Lessons. Every Style.

Guitar is one of the most requested instruments we teach — and one of the most versatile. Rock, country, classical, fingerstyle, blues, pop — students come in wanting to play all of it. Our teachers work with all of it, and with students at every level, from first-timers who have never held a guitar to advanced players preparing for serious performance.

With guitar teachers across all five locations, we give families the best chance of finding a teacher, a schedule, and a location that fits into the week. Every lesson is private, every plan is built around the student in front of the teacher. We do not force every guitar student down the same path. Students work on music they care about while building technique that carries from one style to the next.

All Skill Levels

Where Are You Starting From?

01
Beginner
No experience needed. Basic chords, strumming patterns, simple melodies. Students start playing songs they recognize from the first few lessons — not just exercises. The goal is making music from day one.
02
Intermediate
Students with a foundation expand their chord vocabulary, work on fingerpicking, scales, and music theory. Lessons move beyond beginner songs into the repertoire the student actually wants to build.
03
Advanced
Technical refinement, complex fingerstyle, improvisation, deeper music theory. Students preparing for auditions, competitions, or performance at a serious level get faculty who can take them there.
04
Adult Beginners
Adults who want to learn the songs they have always wanted to play. Rock, country, folk, blues — on your schedule, at your pace, without pressure or artificial milestones.
05
Young Students (Ages 6–9)
Short, focused lessons built around songs kids actually want to learn. Foundational technique comes first — hand position, posture, basic chords — but the music drives engagement from the start.
06
Styles We Teach
Rock, pop, country, blues, classical, fingerstyle, folk, jazz. Students are encouraged to bring the music they love. A teacher who teaches what you want to play keeps students engaged year after year.

01

How Lessons Work

Simple Structure. Clear Expectations.

All guitar lessons are private, one-on-one sessions. Lesson length is chosen based on the student's age, focus, and goals. Younger beginners typically start at 30 minutes. Students building a serious repertoire benefit from longer sessions.

Lessons run seven days a week across our five locations. Siblings can schedule back-to-back at the same location — a practical detail that matters when you are coordinating multiple kids and a full week.
