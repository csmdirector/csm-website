Teacher Makeup Teaching Day Request

For CSM teachers requesting an approved Makeup Teaching Day. The office reviews each request and confirms before any family communication.

Before You Submit

Makeup Teaching Days are additional availability for students who already have makeup credits on their account. They are not a replacement for a cancelled lesson, a teacher absence, or a closure.

Teachers may request up to two (2) Makeup Teaching Days per calendar year. The proposed date must be within 14 days of today.

Do not contact families about your request until the office has confirmed it.

Requests are not guaranteed to be approved. Makeup Teaching Days are a courtesy scheduling option extended to teachers by CSM, based on studio availability and room or instrument resources. Submitting this form is a request, not a confirmation.

Purpose

Makeup Teaching Days provide occasional additional availability for students who currently have makeup credits on their account. These days help reduce accumulated makeup balances while maintaining consistent scheduling procedures across all CSM locations.

Makeup Teaching Days are not used to replace missed lessons from teacher absences or compensate for closures.

Teacher Absences

Teacher absences follow the standard CSM absence process:

  • The absence is logged.
  • The office issues the appropriate tuition credit or refund when applicable.
  • Families are notified by the office.

Students do not receive makeup credits for teacher absences. Teachers do not schedule replacement dates for cancelled lessons.

Makeup Teaching Day Limit

Teachers may request up to two (2) Makeup Teaching Days per calendar year. Approval is not automatic and depends on studio availability and operational considerations.

Separation from Teacher Absences

Makeup Teaching Days may not be requested in connection with a specific teacher absence. Teachers should not propose, schedule, or offer makeup teaching days as a replacement for cancelled lessons. All teacher absences continue to follow the standard CSM absence process.

Use of Makeup Teaching Days

Makeup Teaching Days may be used only for students who already have makeup credits on their account. These days:

  • do not create new makeup credits
  • do not change tuition or billing
  • do not replace missed lessons
  • follow the standard cancellation policy

Restrictions

Makeup Teaching Days may not:

  • be tied to a specific teacher absence
  • be presented as compensation for a missed lesson
  • be scheduled within 30 days of an unexpected studio-wide closure
  • be communicated to families before approval

Scheduling Window

Makeup Teaching Days may only be scheduled within 14 days of the proposed date. Requests submitted earlier than 14 days will not be approved. This ensures accurate room availability and reduces last-minute cancellations.

Request Process

Teachers must submit a Makeup Teaching Day request through the standard request process. Requests must include:

  • proposed date
  • location
  • time block

Management will review requests based on studio availability, operational impact, and scheduling demand. Once approved, the office schedules the day through Opus. Teachers do not schedule students directly.

Communication with Families

When referring to these days, staff and teachers should describe them as: "Additional availability for students with existing makeup credits." They should not be described as making up a missed lesson from a teacher absence or weather closure.

Policy Goal

This structure ensures consistent scheduling procedures across locations while providing occasional additional opportunities for students to redeem existing makeup credits. Makeup Teaching Days are intended to function as an occasional pressure-release opportunity, not a routine expectation.
